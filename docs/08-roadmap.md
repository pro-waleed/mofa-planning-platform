# 08 Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP Foundation

Delivered in this repository:

- Arabic RTL institutional shell
- demo role-based access
- dynamic template engine
- plan creation and tree editing
- KPI registry
- monitoring cycles
- mission reporting flow
- training workflow
- knowledge library
- approvals and notifications
- Neon and Vercel deployment preparation

## Phase 2: Operational Hardening

- production-grade authentication and SSO
- full RBAC and organizational scoping
- audit and compliance expansion
- file uploads and evidence management
- stronger notification delivery channels
- automated migration and seeding pipeline

## Phase 3: Advanced Planning And Analytics

- drag-and-drop hierarchy management
- richer template field designer
- KPI trend history and time-series reporting
- scorecards by unit and strategic theme
- executive export packs and print layouts

## Phase 4: Institutional Scale

- multilingual support where needed
- inter-ministry coordination flows
- portfolio and budget alignment
- advanced search and document versioning
- public-policy research workflows

## Suggested GitHub Milestones

- `M1 - MVP foundation and deployment`
- `M2 - authentication and permissions`
- `M3 - workflow hardening and attachments`
- `M4 - analytics and reporting`
- `M5 - production readiness`

# 01 Product Overview

## Product Name

MOFA Planning Platform

## Institutional Context

This platform is built for the General Directorate of Planning and Research at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Expatriates of the Republic of Yemen.

It serves as an Arabic-first institutional workspace for planning, KPI management, monitoring and evaluation, mission reporting, training and capacity building, approvals, knowledge management, and executive oversight.

## Problem Statement

Institutional planning and follow-up are often spread across spreadsheets, disconnected documents, email-based approvals, and inconsistent reporting formats across departments and diplomatic missions. This makes it difficult to:

- standardize planning structures
- track progress across initiatives and KPIs
- consolidate mission reports into executive insights
- monitor delayed items and corrective actions
- manage approval workflows transparently
- preserve institutional knowledge

## Product Vision

Provide a single Arabic-native platform where institutional planning is modeled dynamically, execution is tracked continuously, and leadership can make decisions from timely and credible information.

## Product Principles

- Arabic-first, RTL-native, and stakeholder-ready
- Dynamic planning engine instead of hardcoded strategic hierarchies
- Executive readability over dashboard clutter
- Workflow-aware and approval-friendly
- Ready for real deployment on GitHub, Neon, and Vercel
- Strong seed data and demo flows for stakeholder presentations

## Primary User Groups

- Platform administrators
- Director General and executive leadership
- Department managers
- Planning analysts
- Monitoring and evaluation officers
- Training officers
- Mission users
- Read-only observers and advisors

## MVP Scope

The MVP implements:

- demo role-based access context
- executive dashboard
- dynamic template engine
- planning module with tree editor
- initiative tracking
- KPI registry
- monitoring cycles and updates
- mission reporting flow
- training workflow
- knowledge document library
- approvals queue
- notifications and recent activity

## Demonstration Outcomes

The MVP is designed to show these institutional flows clearly:

- template creation by an administrator or planner
- plan creation from a dynamic template
- hierarchical plan editing with ownership and KPI linking
- submission and approval routing
- monitoring updates and corrective action capture
- mission report submission and return workflow
- training publication, nomination, and approval
- executive dashboard review by leadership
